Coding Projects

Frenz

Frenz is an app that enables Yale students to meet other students on campus. Users can send virtual gifts, look-up users, send private messages, and view student profiles.

This app utilizes mainly Swift for the front-end (and a big portion of the back-end), typescript to enable push notifications and query random users, and Firebase as the database.

PickPackGo

PickPackGo is a service that enables users to have their packages sent to a local business for the fraction of a P.O. box's price. Not only does it offer affordable pricing, but it provides flexible options based on one's purchasing habits (pay-per-package, 1-month and 3-month subscription plans).

This app utilizes Swift, Javascript, and Firebase as the databse. Click here to view more!

Pirx

Pirx is an app that allows Yale students to post about events currently happening on campus. Users can specify the date/time, post a description, attach a flyer image, and view them on an interactive map.

This app utilizes Swift and Firebase as the database. Click here to view more!
